Tiger

Tigers are the largest cat species in the world reaching up to 3.3 meters.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Length: 2.8 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 8 years
  • Habitat: rain forests, grasslands

A Tiger is a powerful carnivore, a hunter with sharp teeth, strong jaws, and a very agile body. They are the largest naturally occurring species of cat, of which the Siberian tiger is the largest.
